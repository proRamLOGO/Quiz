This C++ application "QUIZZE TIME" is a small program developed in C++ language for Internshala C/C++ Content Writing Internship. This aplication has been developed by Shubh Bansal. A B. Tech CSE student of Maharaja Agrasen INstitute Of Technology. In this quiz 10 questions will be asked from a bank of 30 questions which are picked from different domains of daily life such as GK, News, sports e.t.c. The quiz follows a strict yet simple non-negative marking scheme.
